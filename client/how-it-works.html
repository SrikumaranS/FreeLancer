For Freelancers

Follow these simple steps to start finding work and growing your freelance business.

Create Your Profile

Sign up and create a compelling profile that showcases your skills, experience, and portfolio. A complete profile increases your chances of getting hired.

  • Add a professional profile picture
  • Write a clear and concise bio
  • List your skills and expertise
  • Showcase your best work samples
  • Set your hourly rate or project pricing

Find Projects

Browse through thousands of job postings that match your skills and interests. Use our advanced search and filtering tools to find the perfect projects.

  • Search by skills, budget, and job type
  • Save your favorite searches
  • Get job alerts for new postings
  • Review client ratings and feedback
  • Check project details and requirements

Submit Proposals

Submit compelling proposals to projects you're interested in. Stand out by demonstrating your understanding of the client's needs and how you can help.

  • Write personalized proposals
  • Highlight relevant experience
  • Provide clear timelines and deliverables
  • Ask thoughtful questions
  • Include relevant work samples

Get Hired & Work

Once hired, use our platform to communicate with clients, share files, track time, and get paid securely for your work.

  • Use our messaging system to communicate
  • Share files and updates through the platform
  • Track your time with our time tracker
  • Submit work for client approval
  • Get paid securely through our payment system

For Clients

Follow these simple steps to find and hire the perfect freelancer for your project.

Post a Project

Create a detailed project posting that clearly explains your requirements, budget, and timeline. The more specific you are, the better proposals you'll receive.

  • Write a clear project title
  • Describe your project in detail
  • Specify required skills
  • Set your budget and timeline
  • Add any relevant files or examples

Review Freelancers

Browse freelancer profiles or review proposals submitted to your project. Compare skills, experience, ratings, and portfolios to find the best match.

  • View freelancer profiles and portfolios
  • Check ratings and reviews
  • Compare proposals and pricing
  • Interview top candidates
  • Check work history and experience

Hire the Best

Select the freelancer that best fits your needs and budget. Use our platform to communicate, share files, and manage the project.

  • Send a hiring offer
  • Agree on terms and milestones
  • Fund the project escrow
  • Use our messaging system to communicate
  • Share files and feedback through the platform

Pay Securely

Pay freelancers securely through our platform only when you're satisfied with the work. Our payment protection ensures you only pay for work you approve.

  • Funds held securely in escrow
  • Pay by milestone or project completion
  • Review work before releasing payment
  • Multiple payment methods available
  • Get invoices and receipts

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using FreelanceHub.

How do I create a strong freelancer profile?

A strong profile includes a professional photo, detailed bio, comprehensive skills list, portfolio samples, and clear pricing information. Highlight your unique value proposition and include any relevant certifications or education. Regularly update your profile with new skills and work samples.

What payment methods are supported?

FreelanceHub supports various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and digital wallets. Payments are processed securely through our platform, and we offer payment protection for both clients and freelancers.

How does the escrow payment system work?

When a client hires a freelancer, they fund the project by depositing money into our secure escrow account. The funds are held until the work is completed and approved by the client. Once satisfied, the client releases payment to the freelancer. This system protects both parties.

What fees does FreelanceHub charge?

For freelancers, we charge a 10% service fee on all earnings. For clients, there's a 3% payment processing fee. There are no membership fees or hidden charges. Our pricing is transparent and competitive in the industry.

How do I resolve disputes with freelancers/clients?

We encourage open communication to resolve issues directly. If that fails, our dispute resolution team can mediate. For escrow projects, we can help determine fair payment distribution based on work completed. Always document agreements and communications for reference.
